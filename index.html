CHRIS MEYER

Record Maker
Audio Engineer / Producer / Mixing Engineer / Mastering

Chris Meyer is an American recording engineer, producer, mix engineer, mastering engineer, consultant and educator. He has worked in the audio industry for over a decade and a half. Chris earned credits working with multiple Grammy winning and Multi-Platinum selling producers and engineers across multiple genres (Rock, Pop, Jazz/Funk). As well as working closely with a legendary award winning indie producer. Chris has worked on a song for the soundtrack of a blockbuster Hollywood movie franchise and has also helped independent musicians fulfill their musical vision!
