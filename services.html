SERVICES

MIXING* (online)

When I mix a song, I do my utmost to fulfill the artist’s vision by serving the song and helping to bring the production to life in a way that evokes an emotion from the listener. Allow me to help you fulfill your musical vision by mixing your art. I am available to mix your full album, your new single, or anything in between. Mastering is included.

For a quote, please email me with the following information so we can discuss your project further…

  • Your name
  • Name of the band / artist
  • Number of songs you need mixed
  • Roughly how many tracks the song/s contain/s
  • Approximate length of the song/s
  • Brief description of your project
  • Deadline (if applicable)
  • Any additional information or questions you may have
  • Attach the rough mix (if available)

MASTERING* (online)

Mastering is the final step in the record making process that adds the final bit of enhancement, overall volume, and quality control to a mix that you are already happy with. I am available to master singles or entire albums.

For a quote, please email me with the following information so we can discuss your project further…

  • Your name
  • Name of the band / artist
  • Number of songs you need mastered
  • Approximate length of the song/s
  • Brief description of your project
  • Deadline (if applicable)
  • Any additional information or questions you may have
  • Attach a mix of one of the song/s to master (if available)

Note: Stem mastering is also available. If you would like me to stem master your song/s please mention it in your email.

PRODUCING*** (in-person only)

The number one most important thing is the song. Every single decision made throughout the entirety of the record making process must be made for the benefit of the song. My job as a producer is to help your song evoke an emotion from the listener which then lays the groundwork for an emotional connection being made. Allow me to use my years of experience in the studio to get the best out of you and your music and to guide you through the record making process. Most often I engineer the music that I produce.

For a quote, please email me with the following information so we can discuss your project further…

  • Your name
  • Name of the band / artist
  • Number of songs you need produced
  • Your location & preferred project location
  • Brief description of your project
  • Deadline / preferred time frame
  • Any additional information or questions you may have
  • Attach a demo of the songs (if available)

EDITING (online)

I can provide digital editing to your project. Services offered are, but not limited to… time-aligning drums, tightening vocal doubles / backing vocals, and vocal tuning. These services are not included with my mixing packages, they are add-ons. Email me for more information or to inquire about other editing services.
